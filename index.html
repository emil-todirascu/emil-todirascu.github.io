Welcome to my personal website. My name is Emil and I made this website for anyone interested to find out more about myself. Here, you will learn about my interests, my work experience and education as well as my personal projects.

ABOUT ME

I'm currently studying for a Bachelor's degree majoring in Business & Information Technology at the University of Twente in the Netherlands. I enjoy programming, staying up to date with the economy and financial markets as well as playing video games.

I've been programming for over 3 years fairly regularly out of passion. I have also used it for school work and studied multiple technologies such as JavaScript, SQL or Haskell; but my favourites are still Python, Java and HTML + CSS. I also do coding challenges from time to time and as a result I have managed to reach a couple of milestones that I am very proud of:

  • - Accumulated over 1000 Honor Points on Codewars; placing me in the top 5% of all users.
  • - LeetCode...
  • - AlgoExpert...

From a fairly young age I started to be interested in economics and the financial markets. I like reading about the economy, news about specific companies and new technologies. As a result I have also studied the stock market and educated myself about how it works. I am truly fascinated by it and appreciate how easily accessible it is.

When it comes to video games, I started playing when I was just 9 years old. I initially started with handheld consoles and then later on moved to PC gaming. I have played various games, ranging from action and adventure single players to first person shooters, MOBAs and auto battlers. Some of my all time favourites are: Portal 1 and 2, Red Dead Redemption 2, APB: Reloaded, Team Fortress 2, League of Legends and Teamfight Tactics. I enjoy playing a lot and hope that one day I may be able to work on such a game.
